Thanks for installing Figtree.

Figtree is a simple Chrome extension that lets you quickly copy links to Figma files, pages, and frames. Stay in the flow while writing feedback, giving direction, and referencing your team's projects.

Share exactly what you need.

Quickly copy direct links to any layer in your Figma projects without leaving your current tab.

Pin important items.

Pin frequently accessed frames, pages, and projects for instant access.

Search your files.

Find specific pages, frames, and groups in your Figma files instantly with powerful search functionality.

Stay local.

All data is cached locally and never sent to the cloud. Figma access is read-only.

See into your projects.

Visual previews of frames and groups help you quickly identify the right content.
